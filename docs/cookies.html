1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are used to remember preferences, analyze traffic, and improve the user experience.

2. What cookies do we use?

On kdashboard.app we use the following types of cookies:

CookieTypeDurationPurposeOwner

_ga_*Analytics2 yearsMaintains session state in GA4Third-party (Google)

3. Legal basis

Technical cookies (cookie_consent, theme) are based on the legitimate interest of the website's operation. Analytics cookies (Google Analytics) are only installed with your prior and explicit consent, in accordance with the GDPR (Article 6.1.a).

4. How to revoke consent?

You can change your cookie preference at any time by clicking the button below. You can also delete cookies from your browser settings.
