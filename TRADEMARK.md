separate; this document covers the trademark side.

## Uses that do not require permission

You may use the Marks, without asking first, for any of the following:

1. Referring to the kdashboard project in articles, tutorials, blog posts,
   videos, conference talks, books, academic papers, or social media.
2. Indicating compatibility: stating that your tool works with kdashboard, or
   that kdashboard works with your tool.
3. Linking to the official kdashboard website, repository, or downloads.
4. Unmodified redistribution of official binaries, provided you do not alter
   the name, logo, or branding and do not imply endorsement.
5. Community events, meetups, and user groups, provided they are not-for-profit
   and clearly identify themselves as community-run (for example,
   "kdashboard Berlin Meetup").

When referring to the project in prose, please use "kdashboard" (lowercase
"k"), not "KDashboard" or "K-Dashboard".

## Uses that require written permission

You must obtain written permission from Folio Innova before any of the
following:

1. Using the Marks in the name of a product, service, company, domain name,
   social media account, or mobile application.
2. Selling merchandise (T-shirts, stickers, mugs, etc.) bearing the Marks.
3. Modifying the logo or creating derivative logos.
4. Any use that could suggest sponsorship, affiliation, or endorsement by
   Folio Innova.
5. Distributing a modified fork or build of the software under the name
   "kdashboard" or a confusingly similar name.

Forks and derivative works are welcome under the terms of the license, but
they must be released under a different name.

## Requesting permission

Send trademark-related inquiries to `trademark@kdashboard.io`. Include:

- Your name or organization
- A description of the intended use
- Mockups or examples if applicable
- Expected reach or scale of distribution

Typical response time is two weeks.

## Enforcement

Folio Innova reserves all rights in the Marks and will take action against
uses that violate this policy or cause public confusion. Most enforcement
issues are resolved informally, starting with a request to change or stop
the unauthorized use.

## Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. The version in effect at the
time of your use applies. Material changes will be announced in the project
changelog.
